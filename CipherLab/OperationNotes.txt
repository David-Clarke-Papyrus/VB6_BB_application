Notes for using product search facility on Cipherlab device
============================================================

1. Start the device. (Button on bottom right)

2. Scan the barcode of the product sought (point and use big yellow button).

3. Scan each potential product match until the device emits a long beep and lights up. This indicates a match is made.

4. You can either 

	a) turn off device (button on bottom right)
	b) start new search (press "1" and 'Enter' which is the right-hand blue button)
	c) continue the search (press "2" and 'Enter' which is the right-hand blue button)
